Flood Safety Tips

Evacuation

Move to higher ground immediately if water levels rise quickly. Follow official evacuation instructions.

Road Safety

Do not attempt to drive through flooded roads. Even shallow water can sweep a vehicle away.

Home Safety

Turn off electricity and gas if safe to do so. Avoid walking or wading through floodwater indoors.

Health & Hygiene

Floodwater may be contaminated. Avoid contact and clean wounds immediately if exposed.
