Earthquake Safety Tips

During Shaking

Drop, Cover, and Hold On. Get under a sturdy desk or table and protect your head and neck.

After the Quake

Exit the building carefully and move to an open area away from structures. Be prepared for aftershocks.

Building Safety

Check for structural damage, gas leaks, and electrical issues. Do not enter damaged buildings.

Avoid Hazards

Stay away from windows, heavy furniture, and exterior walls. Watch for falling debris and damaged power lines.
