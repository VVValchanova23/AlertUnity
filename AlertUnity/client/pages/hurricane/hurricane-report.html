Hurricane Safety Tips

Evacuation

Follow evacuation orders immediately. Move inland or to designated shelters.

Wind Safety

Stay indoors and away from windows. Secure loose outdoor items.

Home Safety

Turn off electricity if flooding is likely. Avoid going outside during the storm.

After the Storm

Watch for downed power lines and unstable debris. Do not return home until officials say it's safe.
